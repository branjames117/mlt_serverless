About Us

Murfreesboro Little Theatre is the oldest continuing community theatre in the area, starting in 1962! As a 501(c)(3) Non Profit Organization, we operate on ticket sales and memberships. We strive to provide an eclectic array of shows to entice and entertain everyone!
